# Content Pillars

## Money Saving
Focus:
- Rent negotiation
- Budgeting and affordability
- Cost-cutting strategies

Avoid:
- Guarantees or exaggerated savings claims

## Legal Rights
Focus:
- Tenant protections
- Lease exits and disputes
- Rights education and plain-English explanations

Requirements:
- Use legal disclaimers
- Avoid giving legal advice

## Costs and Fees
Focus:
- Hidden rental fees
- True monthly rent cost
- Deposits, utilities, and recurring add-ons

## Apartment Hunting
Focus:
- First apartment guides
- Moving checklists
- Decision frameworks and timelines

## Technology and Tools
Focus:
- Calculators
- Estimators
- Decision helpers

Requirements:
- Tools must be fast and usable without ads

## Financial Planning
Focus:
- Rent vs buy analysis
- Long-term affordability
- Neutral, educational framing only

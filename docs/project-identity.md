# RentingExplained.com Project Identity

## Purpose
RentingExplained.com is a renter-first education platform that helps people:
- Save money
- Avoid hidden fees
- Understand tenant rights
- Make informed housing decisions

## Non-Negotiables
- Renter-first advocacy and trust above monetization
- Informational, neutral tone (8th to 10th grade reading level)
- No landlord bias, no sales funnel behavior, no affiliate-first framing
- The site must remain useful even with zero ads

## Audience
- Renters and first-time renters
- People facing rent increases, lease questions, or move decisions
- Users looking for clear, actionable guidance

## Voice and Tone
- Plain-English, practical, and calm
- Avoid hype, urgency, and guarantees
- Do not provide legal advice; use educational framing and disclaimers

## Success Definition
- Users can understand their options, protect their rights, and save money
- Content stands alone without ads or gating
- Long-term trust over short-term gains

## Safety and Change Rules
- Preserve trust, accuracy, and renter-first advocacy
- Maintain strict Google AdSense compliance
- If uncertain, analyze and propose before acting

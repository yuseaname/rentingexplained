# SEO Architecture

## URL and Routing Stability
- Preserve all existing URLs
- Do not break routing or canonical structure
- Maintain internal linking integrity

## Site Structure
- Use topic clusters aligned to content pillars
- Keep hubs for Guides, Tools, Costs, and Legal Rights
- Avoid orphaned pages

## On-Page SEO
- Clear titles and meta descriptions
- Descriptive headings and clean hierarchy
- Plain-English writing; avoid keyword stuffing

## Schema Markup
- Use only accurate, appropriate schema
- Never add misleading or spammy markup
- Avoid inflated claims or unsupported data

## Indexing and Sitemaps
- Keep sitemap accurate and current
- Do not block core content in robots
- Prioritize content that provides unique value

## Content Updates
- Refresh time-sensitive content as laws or guidance change
- Keep publish and modified dates accurate

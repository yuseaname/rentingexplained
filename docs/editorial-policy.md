# Editorial Policy

## Mission
Provide accurate, unbiased, renter-first information that helps people make informed renting decisions.

## Standards
- Accuracy first: verify claims with credible sources
- Neutral, educational tone; avoid legal advice language
- No hype, no guarantees, no urgency framing
- Content must stand alone without ads

## Sources
Preferred:
- Government and official legal resources (.gov)
- Reputable academic research
- Established news outlets
- Primary data and official reports

Avoid:
- Anonymous sources without verification
- Low-quality aggregators or content mills
- Promotional or partisan sources

## Review Process
- Research and outline
- Draft with citations
- Fact-check and update dates
- Final review for clarity and bias

## Updates and Corrections
- Review content periodically or when laws change
- Clearly note significant updates
- Correct errors promptly and transparently

## Legal and Financial Disclaimers
- Use educational framing only
- Add disclaimers on legal and regulatory content
- Encourage readers to consult qualified professionals

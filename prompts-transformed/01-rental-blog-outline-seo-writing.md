## PROMPT: RENTAL ADVICE BLOG OUTLINE & SEO-OPTIMIZED ARTICLE WRITING

You are writing for **RentingExplained.com**, an authoritative educational platform that helps renters save money, understand their rights, and make informed housing decisions.

**Target Audience:** Renters aged 22-45, first-time renters, budget-conscious individuals seeking practical rental advice.

**Brand Voice:** Helpful, authoritative but approachable, empowering, practical, educational. Use clear language that demystifies complex rental topics.

**Content Goals:**
- Educate renters with actionable, practical advice
- Rank for long-tail rental-related keywords
- Build trust and authority in the rental advice space
- Drive engagement through quizzes, tools, and interactive elements
- Support affiliate partnerships (rental insurance, moving services, budgeting tools)

---

### TASK 1: CREATE SEO-OPTIMIZED BLOG OUTLINE

**Your Task:** Create a comprehensive blog outline for a rental advice article.

**Requirements:**

1. **Topic Selection:**
   - Focus on topics within these pillars:
     * Money-saving strategies for renters
     * Tenant rights and legal information
     * Rental costs and hidden fees
     * Apartment hunting and moving
     * Tools and apps for renters
   
2. **Keyword Research:**
   - Identify 1 primary keyword (e.g., "how to save money on rent")
   - Include 3-5 secondary keywords (LSI keywords)
   - Target long-tail keywords renters actually search for

3. **Outline Structure:**
   ```
   I. SEO Title (60 characters max, include primary keyword)
   
   II. Meta Description (155 characters, compelling, includes keyword)
   
   III. Introduction (150-200 words)
       - Hook with relatable renter problem
       - Promise specific value/solution
       - Preview main takeaways
   
   IV. Key Takeaways Box (4-6 bullet points)
       - Quick wins readers will learn
       - Specific numbers/savings when possible
   
   V. Main Content Sections (H2 headers, 6-8 sections)
       For each section:
       - Clear, benefit-driven H2 (include keywords naturally)
       - 3-5 H3 subheadings with specific tactics
       - Include: examples, statistics, actionable steps
       - Suggested word count per section: 250-400 words
   
   VI. Interactive Elements (choose 1-2):
       - Quiz questions related to topic
       - Calculator or tool recommendation
       - Checklist or downloadable resource
       - Real renter scenario/case study
   
   VII. Internal Linking Opportunities (3-5 links)
       - Link to related articles on RentingExplained.com
       - Link to relevant tools/calculators
       - Link to state-specific tenant rights pages
   
   VIII. Affiliate Integration Points (2-3 natural placements)
       - Renters insurance comparison tools
       - Moving service recommendations
       - Budgeting apps or credit monitoring
       - Apartment listing platforms
   
   IX. FAQ Section (Schema-ready)
       - 4-6 common questions renters ask
       - Direct, concise answers (50-100 words each)
   
   X. Conclusion (100-150 words)
       - Recap main savings/benefits
       - Clear call-to-action
       - Encourage engagement (quiz, tool, comments)
   ```

4. **Content Depth:**
   - Target 2,000-2,500 words total
   - Aim for comprehensive, definitive content
   - Include specific numbers, dollar amounts, timeframes

---

### TASK 2: WRITE FULL SEO-OPTIMIZED ARTICLE

Using the approved outline, write the complete article following these guidelines:

**Writing Style:**
- **Tone:** Conversational but authoritative - like a knowledgeable friend helping out
- **Perspective:** Second person ("you") to directly address renters
- **Sentence structure:** Mix of short (impact) and medium (explanation) sentences
- **Avoid:** Legal jargon, corporate speak, passive voice

**Content Requirements:**

1. **Introduction (150-200 words)**
   - Start with a relatable scenario renters face
   - Use specific numbers: "The average renter overpays $200/month..."
   - Promise clear, actionable solutions
   - Preview the savings/benefits they'll learn

2. **Key Takeaways Box** (immediately after intro)
   ```
   Example format:
   ?? Key Takeaways
   ? Save $50-300/month with these 7 proven strategies
   ? Understand your legal rights in all 50 states
   ? Avoid hidden fees that add 15-30% to rent costs
   ? Access free tools to calculate your ideal rent budget
   ```

3. **Main Sections (6-8 H2 sections, 300-400 words each)**
   
   For each section:
   - **H2:** Benefit-driven headline with keyword
   - **Opening:** Why this matters to renters
   - **H3 subsections:** Specific tactics, numbered steps
   - **Examples:** Real scenarios, dollar amounts, percentages
   - **Actionable steps:** "Here's what to do today..."
   
   **Content Elements to Include:**
   - ? Statistics with sources
   - ? Real dollar savings amounts
   - ? Step-by-step instructions
   - ? "Pro tip" boxes or callouts
   - ? Warning boxes for common mistakes
   - ? State-specific information where relevant

4. **Interactive Elements**
   
   Include 1-2 of:
   - **Quiz:** 5-7 questions testing knowledge on topic
   - **Calculator prompt:** "Use our [tool name] to calculate..."
   - **Checklist:** Downloadable PDF or inline checklist
   - **Scenario:** "Meet Sarah, who saved $2,400/year by..."

5. **Internal Linking (3-5 links minimum)**
   
   Link to existing RentingExplained.com content:
   - Related articles in same category
   - Relevant tools/calculators
   - Tenant rights pages
   - Cost calculators
   
   Format: Natural, contextual links
   Example: "Check out our complete guide to [tenant rights in California]"

6. **Affiliate Integration (2-3 placements)**
   
   Naturally recommend:
   - Renters insurance providers
   - Moving services or truck rentals
   - Budgeting apps or financial tools
   - Apartment listing services
   
   **Critical:** Always disclose affiliate relationships
   Format: "We may earn a commission if you use this service (at no cost to you)"

7. **FAQ Section (Schema markup ready)**
   
   Include 4-6 questions formatted as:
   ```
   ### [Question renters commonly search]
   
   Direct answer in 50-100 words. Include specific details, numbers, or steps.
   Link to full article if topic is covered in depth elsewhere.
   ```

8. **Conclusion (100-150 words)**
   - Recap the main savings or benefits
   - Specific call-to-action:
     * "Take our 2-minute quiz to test your knowledge"
     * "Use our free calculator to see your ideal rent budget"
     * "Download our free checklist"
   - Encourage comments/questions

**SEO Optimization:**

- **Primary keyword:** Use in title, first paragraph, 1 H2, conclusion
- **Secondary keywords:** Natural distribution throughout
- **Keyword density:** 1-2% for primary keyword
- **Image alt text:** Descriptive, includes keywords
- **URL slug:** Clean, keyword-rich (e.g., /save-money-renting-apartment)
- **Internal links:** Anchor text varies, keyword-rich but natural

**Technical Requirements:**

- **Word count:** 2,000-2,500 words
- **Reading level:** 8th-9th grade (Flesch-Kincaid)
- **Paragraph length:** Max 3-4 sentences
- **Headings:** H2 and H3 only (no H4+)
- **Lists:** Use bulleted/numbered lists for scanability
- **Bold/emphasis:** Highlight key takeaways, numbers, action items

**Before Submission:**

- [ ] Title includes primary keyword and is under 60 characters
- [ ] Meta description is compelling and under 155 characters
- [ ] Introduction hooks reader and promises specific value
- [ ] Each section provides actionable, specific advice
- [ ] All statistics have sources
- [ ] Internal links are contextual and helpful
- [ ] Affiliate links are disclosed
- [ ] FAQ section uses schema-ready format
- [ ] Conclusion has clear call-to-action
- [ ] Article is 2,000+ words
- [ ] Content is original, helpful, and authoritative

Deliver the article in Markdown format with:
- Proper heading hierarchy (# for H1, ## for H2, ### for H3)
- Inline formatting (bold, italics, links)
- Structured data suggestions for FAQ schema
- Suggested image placements with alt text
- Internal linking suggestions marked clearly

- Receives social shares and backlinks
- Answers renter questions comprehensively

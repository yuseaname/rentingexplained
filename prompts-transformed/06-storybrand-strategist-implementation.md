## PROMPT: STORYBRAND FRAMEWORK IMPLEMENTATION FOR RENTINGEXPLAINED.COM

You are implementing the StoryBrand framework on **RentingExplained.com**, an educational rental advice platform helping renters save money and understand their rights.

**Mission:** Apply Donald Miller's StoryBrand principles to clarify messaging, reduce confusion, and guide renters through a clear journey from problem to solution.

---

## STORYBRAND FRAMEWORK OVERVIEW

The 7-part StoryBrand structure positions the CUSTOMER (renter) as the hero, and RentingExplained.com as the GUIDE helping them succeed.

```
1. A CHARACTER (The Renter)
2. HAS A PROBLEM (Overpaying, confusion, vulnerability)
3. AND MEETS A GUIDE (RentingExplained.com)
4. WHO GIVES THEM A PLAN (Clear steps to save money, understand rights)
5. AND CALLS THEM TO ACTION (Use tools, read guides, sign up)
6. THAT HELPS THEM AVOID FAILURE (Eviction, scams, financial loss)
7. AND ENDS IN A SUCCESS (Saving money, empowered renting, peace of mind)
```

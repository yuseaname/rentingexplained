### PHASE 4: WRITE ALL THREE ARTICLES

Using the approved outlines, write three complete, publication-ready articles.

**Writing Guidelines:**

**1. Brand Voice for RentingExplained.com:**
- Helpful and empowering (never condescending)
- Authoritative but approachable (expert friend, not lawyer)
- Practical and action-oriented (clear next steps)
- Empathetic to renter challenges
- Optimistic about solutions

**2. Content Quality Standards:**

? **Accuracy:**
- All statistics must have credible sources
- Legal information verified for 2025
- Dollar amounts and percentages must be realistic
- State-specific info must be current

? **Depth:**
- Comprehensive coverage of topic
- Addresses objections and edge cases
- Includes beginner and advanced info
- Answers related questions preemptively

? **Actionability:**
- Every section has clear takeaways
- Steps are specific and numbered
- Examples show real application
- Readers can implement immediately

? **Readability:**
- 8th-9th grade reading level
- Short paragraphs (3-4 sentences max)
- Mix of sentence lengths
- Scannable with bullets, numbers, bold text

**3. Required Elements in Each Article:**

Must include:
- [ ] Hook with specific numbers/statistics
- [ ] Key takeaways box
- [ ] At least 1 real example or case study
- [ ] 2-3 "Pro Tip" or "Warning" callout boxes
- [ ] Step-by-step instructions where applicable
- [ ] FAQ section (4-6 questions)
- [ ] Clear call-to-action in conclusion
- [ ] 5+ internal links to RentingExplained.com
- [ ] 2-3 affiliate integrations (with disclosure)
- [ ] Sources cited for all statistics

**4. Formatting Requirements:**

- Use Markdown format
- Heading hierarchy: # H1, ## H2, ### H3
- Bold for emphasis on key points
- Italics for emphasis or examples
- Links formatted as [anchor text](URL)
- Lists: Bulleted for options, numbered for steps
- Blockquotes for testimonials or important notes
